## ğŸ”¬ The Science of Synergy

### Why Combinations Work Better

**1. PS + Omega-3s = Enhanced Membrane Function**
- Phosphatidylserine (PS) forms the membrane structure
- Omega-3s (DHA/EPA) integrate into membranes, improving fluidity
- Together: Better neuronal signaling, improved attention
- *Studies show PS-DHA conjugates have superior bioavailability*

**2. Zinc + B6 (P5P) = Neurotransmitter Support**
- Both are cofactors for dopamine and serotonin synthesis
- Zinc activates B6 to its active form (P5P)
- Together: Enhanced neurotransmitter production
- *Classic combination in functional medicine for ADHD*

**3. NAC + Glycine = Glutathione Boost**
- NAC provides cysteine (rate-limiting precursor)
- Glycine is another glutathione building block
- Together: Up to 200% increase in glutathione synthesis
- *Addresses oxidative stress common in ASD/OCD*

**4. Vitamin D + Omega-3s = Enhanced Absorption**
- Vitamin D is fat-soluble
- Omega-3s provide optimal fat matrix
- Together: Better vitamin D uptake and utilization

**5. Iron + Vitamin C = 3x Better Absorption**
- Vitamin C converts iron to more absorbable form
- Also protects against GI side effects
- *Critical for addressing ADHD-related iron deficiency*

**6. Probiotics + Prebiotic Fiber = Colonization Success**
- Prebiotics feed beneficial bacteria
- Improves probiotic survival and function
- Together: Better gut-brain axis support

## âš ï¸ Critical Interactions & Warnings

### SEPARATE THESE (4+ hours apart):
| Supplement A | Supplement B | Why |
|--------------|--------------|-----|
| Zinc | Iron | Compete for absorption |
| Zinc | Copper | Zinc depletes copper long-term |
| Calcium | Iron | Calcium blocks iron absorption |
| NAC | Activated charcoal | Charcoal binds NAC |

### USE CAUTION - Discuss with Doctor:
| Combination | Concern |
|-------------|---------|
| Saffron + SSRIs | Both increase serotonin â†’ serotonin syndrome risk |
| High-dose omega-3s + blood thinners | Increased bleeding risk |
| NAC + nitroglycerin | Potentiates effects |
| Iron + Levothyroxine | Iron blocks thyroid med absorption |
| Zinc >40mg/day long-term | Can deplete copper |

### ABSOLUTE CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- **NAC**: Active bleeding disorders, upcoming surgery (stop 2 weeks before)
- **Iron**: Hemochromatosis, iron overload conditions
- **Folinic acid**: Without FRAA testing/indication

## ğŸš€ Getting Started: Week-by-Week Guide

### Week 1-2: Foundation Start
- Start omega-3s at half dose, increase to full by end of week 2
- Add vitamin D3/K2
- Observe tolerance

### Week 3-4: Complete Foundation
- Add magnesium (evening)
- Add probiotics (morning, empty stomach)
- Add prebiotic fiber (with meals)

- Choose ONE supplement from your relevant Tier 2 category

- Add one more from Tier 2
- Continue observing

### Week 9-12: Optimize
- Fine-tune doses based on response
- Add remaining Tier 2 supplements as tolerated
- Consider Tier 3 after 3 months
